What is Pine Nut Brittle?

Pine nut brittle is a gourmet candy made with caramelized sugar and buttery pine nuts. It’s a crunchy, sweet, and slightly nutty treat perfect for holidays, gifts, or everyday indulgence.

Basic Pine Nut Brittle Recipe

  1. Heat sugar until it caramelizes into golden syrup.
  2. Stir in pine nuts and optional flavorings.
  3. Spread onto parchment paper and let cool.
  4. Break into pieces and enjoy!

Popular Variations

  • Rosemary Infused: Add a hint of fresh rosemary.
  • Spicy Kick: Mix in chili flakes or cayenne pepper.
  • Citrus Zest: Brighten with lemon or orange zest.

Storage & Serving Tips

Store pine nut brittle in an airtight container at room temperature. Use it as a snack, gift, or topping for desserts like ice cream or yogurt.
